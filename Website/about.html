About Teen 2 Tycoon


Teen 2 Tycoon (T2T) is an entrepreneurial development and youth innovation platform established with the vision to nurture young minds into future business leaders. Founded and directed by Mr. Ilyas Ansari, the organization is powered by a team of 22 teenage co-founders who represent the next generation of innovators, creators, and change-makers.


T2T stands as a revolutionary youth-led initiative in India, bridging the gap between traditional education and practical entrepreneurship. The platform introduces real-world business exposure, leadership experiences, and innovation challenges to students from an early age, helping them transform ideas into enterprises.

CEO Statement

Mr. Ilyas Ansari

Founder & Director, Teen 2 Tycoon

"Our schools teach us how to follow; T2T teaches how to lead."

A visionary entrepreneur and hotelier, Mr. Ansari began his professional journey with international experience at Fairmont Grand Del Mar, San Diego, USA, in Room Division Management. His exposure to global hospitality and management inspired him to return to India with a mission to foster entrepreneurial skills among young minds.


Through his leadership, T2T has evolved into a multi-vertical platform integrating event management, social media strategy, and entrepreneurial education — all centered on empowering the youth to build businesses from a young age.

Our Objectives

To redefine India's education-to-employment pipeline

Vision

To redefine India's education-to-employment pipeline by producing employers, not employees, and building a community of young entrepreneurs who drive sustainable change.

Mission

To transform students into confident, skilled, and future-ready individuals by integrating entrepreneurship, creativity, and practical learning with education.

Goal

To cultivate entrepreneurial thinking and leadership qualities among teenagers by providing a platform that encourages creativity, innovation, and action-driven learning.

Impact

To create a generation of job creators instead of job seekers, empowering youth to build sustainable businesses and contribute to India's economic growth.
