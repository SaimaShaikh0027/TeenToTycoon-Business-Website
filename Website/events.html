SkillFest

Mumbai's Premier Student-Centric Competitive Festival

SkillFest is one of Mumbai's most anticipated student-centric competitive festivals, created to celebrate skills, creativity, curiosity, and excellence among young learners. The festival provides a powerful platform for children and students to showcase their talents across academics, sports, innovation, and creative disciplines.

Founded in 2013 at Ghatkopar by Prof. Pradeep (Druv Sir) Yadav, SkillFest has grown into a city-level celebration attracting participation from schools and institutions across Mumbai and Navi Mumbai. The fest entered a new phase of structured growth and modern execution under the leadership of Mr. Ilyas Ansari, who successfully led the 2021–2022 editions, strengthening student engagement and expanding the festival's reach.

With a diverse range of indoor, outdoor, and online competitions, SkillFest ensures inclusive participation for all. Every participant is recognized with a certificate, while winners are honored with medals, merit recognitions, and select cash prizes. With thousands of students participating each year, SkillFest continues to empower the future by turning talent into confidence and opportunity.

T2T Summer Camp

School Students | Skill-Based Training Program

T2T Summer Camp is a structured, skill-based training program designed for school students, aligned with the National Education Policy (NEP 2020) and STEM principles. This exclusive summer program offers 26 creative and skill-based activities focused on innovation, leadership, and entrepreneurial mindset building.

The camp focuses on experiential learning and practical exposure, helping students explore interests, build confidence, and develop future-ready skills beyond academics in a fun, engaging, and impactful environment.

Post Board Exam Celebration

Euforic - 26

After Exam Celebration

Euforic is a curated celebration event designed exclusively for students after SSC and HSC Board Examinations. It provides a safe, positive, and refreshing environment where students can de-stress and rejuvenate after exams, engage in fun activities, entertainment, games, and performances, and network with peers while celebrating academic milestones.

This event balances enjoyment with motivation, helping students reset their mindset before stepping into the next academic or career phase. It's a perfect blend of entertainment, relaxation, and inspirational sessions that prepare students for their future journey.

Career & Stream Orientation

Dream Campus

Career & Stream Orientation for Std. XI Students

Dream Campus is a career-orientation and exposure initiative designed for Std. XI aspirants. The program offers insight into academic streams, career paths, and future opportunities, guidance from educators, mentors, and industry experts, and exposure to colleges, courses, skills, and career ecosystems.

Dream Campus empowers students to make informed decisions by aligning interests, aptitude, and career goals—reducing confusion and building clarity at a crucial transition stage. This program helps students understand various career options and choose the right path for their future.
